About the Role:

=20

ClosedLoop is looking for a customer-facing Data Scientist to build and deploy predictive models for our healthcare customers. ClosedLoop data scientists utilize our platform and work alongside our customer's data science teams to deliver machine learning models into real world healthcare settings. In addition to ensuring the quality of the models, you will also help customers understand how to integrate those models into their processes and to measure the effectiveness of the models in production.

=20
