About the job

BICP, a leader in the delivery of innovative data analytics & science products for large enterprise customers is hiring a Data Scientist to join our data products delivery team at a Fortune 100 retail client in Portland, OR. Our DS will be part of a Data Products Delivery squad focused primarily on delivering new insights associated with Fulfillment & Supply Chain metrics. Data Scientist will assist in the definition of new insights based on stakeholder specifications, scope and estimate ROI for each opportunity and scale systems to enable the delivery of these existing & new data products. We are looking for candidates with excellent communication, interpersonal and documentation skills in addition to proven success collaborating effectively with key business stakeholders we well as highly technical development teams. Ideal candidates will have deep SQL expertise with end-to-end data experience including discovery, extraction, manipulation, blending, and visualization.


We are looking for smart, analytical people with a unique blend of technical aptitude and the polish required to partner with the business and deliver accurate and meaningful insights. What you will support:

  • Support the expansion of our Lifetime Value Model (LTV) to include supply chain metrics and variables.
  • Use data to predicts trends and perform statistical analysis that can identify correlations and patterns between Supply chain Metrics (Split Shipment rate, Delivery Satisfaction, Carrier Performance, ETC.)
  • Assist in conducting Adhoc analysis & data needs, business case development, & deliverable creation
  • Establish a easy functional way to conduct A/B analysis for testing using Optimizly
  • Develop SQL queries and data visualizations to fulfill ad-hoc analysis requests and ongoing reporting needs leveraging standard query syntax



What you bring to the table:

  • Tableau Data Visualization
