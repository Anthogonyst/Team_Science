About the job

Title: Data Scientist

Location: 100% remote

Type: Direct Hire

Compensation: 125 to 145k and Benefits


The real key to this position:

***Must have AI/ML model development in a manufacturing environment, everything else is a plus


Data Scientists are responsible for participating in the implementation of continuous improvement projects using the D-M-A-I-C methodology of Six Sigma. Data Scientists leverage Data Science and Artificial Intelligence/Machine Learning (AI) tools to further data driven decision making across the company and influence positive change. By defining a problem with an unknown solution, measuring the current process capability and collecting data, analyzing relevant data, optimizing the process, and controlling key process outputs.


Essential Job Functions

  • Utilizing Six Sigma principles with data science and AI tools, participate in and complete projects, analyses, reports, and visualizations in an accurate and timely fashion.
  • Provide support to teams by providing them with the information, analysis, training and tools to succeed; promote and demonstrate effective and "boundaryless=E2=80=9D communication to achieve "cross pollination=E2=80=9D in all areas, at all levels.
  • Identify, obtain, explore, clean, transform and analyze project data; Select, develop, train, evaluate, and explain machine learning models; and integrate, deploy, operate, and maintain Client models.
  • Lead and guide others in the practical use of the Six Sigma philosophy and Data Science tools.
  • Concentrate on never-ending improvement.
  • Utilize teamwork and apply team concepts as a member of project teams and, as possible, in the day to day operation of the facility.
  • Travel up to 15% of the time.
  • Conduct and oversee Data Science training, as needed.


Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree in data science, computer science, math, science or engineering, 2 + years related work experience and/or training, or equivalent combination of education and experience.
  • Understanding of advanced data science techniques for Supervised and Unsupervised Learning methods such as Classification, Regression, and Clustering Analysis.
  • Proficient in Data Visualization to analyze and represent data and insights to project team, management, and end users.
  • Ability to read, analyze and interpret general business periodicals, professional journals, technical procedures or governmental regulations.
  • Ability to draft reports, business correspondence and procedure manuals.
  • Ability to effectively present information and respond to questions from groups of managers, clients, and customers.
  • Ability to apply mathematical operations to such tasks as frequency distribution, determination of test reliability and validity, analysis of variance, correlation techniques, sampling theory and factor analysis.
  • Ability to define problems, collect data, establish facts and draw valid conclusions.
  • Ability to interpret an extensive variety of technical instructions in mathematical or diagram form and deal with several abstract and concrete variables.
  • Understanding of advanced Six Sigma and statistical process control concepts, including control charts, variation, cause and effect relationships, Pareto principle (vital few/useful many) and process capability.
  • Experience working with SQL, Power BI, SAP, MiniTab, RapidMiner, R, Python, or similar tools.
