About the job

Consultant, Bioinformatics Developer, Pharmaceutical

Summary

A key role for the organization, this individual will be responsible for working with our Biomarker Discovery and IT teams on a daily basis.

To succeed in this role, the individual will need to understand the purpose of genomics in the drug development process and while not required to analyze results, the right candidate will be able to engage with scientists to understand the problem statement and develop workflows that support analysis of genomics data.

As DNAnexus is the primary commercial platform used by the team, the candidate ideally will have had experience working with this platform to support analyses. With the guidance of our scientists and IT, this individual will be responsible for developing workflows through software development / programming using python, WDL, and by leveraging platforms such as AWS and DNAnexus. As there are many open source and web-based tools available to support genomic analysis, this individual will work with IT and scientists to onboard these tools and automate the analysis processes needed by the team.

Responsibilities

  • Work closely with Bioanalytical scientists to develop relevant workflows and data pipelines
  • Analyze, understand and translate business requirements
  • Develop workflows in support of genomics analysis on the DNAnexus platform
  • Coordinate with vendor, infrastructure, project and IT support team members
  • Excellent communication and presentation skills
  • Excellent organizational, time management and follow through skills


Skills & Experience

  • 2+ years of knowledge leveraging the DNAnexus platform and products (e.g. dxToolkit)
  • 3+ years of experience in python programming
  • 3+ years of experience developing data pipelines for genomics
  • Ability to develop genomics workflows e.g. using Workflow Description Language (WDL)
  • Awareness of AWS cloud analytics & genomics
  • 2+ years of experience working across IT and business teams in a matrixed environment
  • 3+ years of knowledge and understanding of Biomarker, Bioanalytical and Genomics Research


Terms & Start

  • 12 month contract with ability to extend
  • Remote to start - some travel in the future to Rockville, MD
  • Healthcare Benefits available
  • Start ASAP
