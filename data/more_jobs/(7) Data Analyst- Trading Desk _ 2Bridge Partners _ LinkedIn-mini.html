About the job

NYC Mortgage Trading Desk seeks a Data Analyst with strong Python and SQL experience. Mortgage industry experience is a plus though not required.


This is a permanent position on-site in NYC.


Compensation includes base, bonus, pto, 401k, medical, dental, vision, and more!



The Residential Agency Mortgage Trading Desk manages risk on residential agency mortgages, from the time that the rate is locked for the borrower, to securitization and sale of resulting MBS.


Responsibilities


  • Assist the head trader in managing the day to day operations of the business
  • Data analysis
  • SQL programming
  • Creating custom SSRS reports
  • Python programming


Requirements


  • Master's Degree in Science/Engineering or Management Information Systems
  • Previous experience in a similar role, especially as an Analyst on a Trading Desk, is preferred
  • Mortgage experience
  • Experience with relational databases, preferably Microsoft SQL Server
  • Experience writing SQL queries
  • Experience with Python programming
