About the job

At intermeta, we are working on the next evolution of the game engine. Right now, we are looking for passionate and skilled individuals to work with our team on building the foundation for our product and service line. Interest in working in a startup environment is a must, and passion for the project is paramount.


In particular, we are looking for people with:

  • Expertise in C++ and experience in C# (ideally 3-5 years, but we are mostly looking for demonstration of skill).
  • 2D and/or 3D Game engine development experience (3D required for long term opportunities).


Other valuable skills are:

  • OpenGL and comparable programs.
  • Experience with using Unity and other engines.


We are also hoping applicants will be:

  • Passionate about the metaverse space.
  • Interest in working with our team on cryptocurrency integration.
  • Good with interpersonal skills, especially with our remote and virtual team.


This is a part time job that will lead to full time employment, so it is important that candidates exert strong effort even before full employment is available. Responsibilities will include:

  • Working with our team on experimenting with and building a functional game engine prototype.
  • Project planning and execution.
  • Working with our engineers with various areas of expertise to bring lots of creativity and ingenuity to our project.


Candidates should expect a startup experience with flexible payment terms and close proximity to the inner workings of the company. This position comes with great opportunity, the chance to be part of something much bigger than just a part time job; show us you can change the world with our team!


**Applicants able to work in person 1-2 days per month in the DC area will be very strongly considered**
