Location: New York, NY

Salary: Base up to 135k

Visa sponsorship is NOT available

  • Create processes for monitoring model precision and data accuracy
  • Implement data quality and KPI monitoring standards

  • 2+ years experience developing predictive models, preferably to increase customer lifetime value and reduce marketing costs
