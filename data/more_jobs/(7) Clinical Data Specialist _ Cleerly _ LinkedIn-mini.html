About the Opportunity


We have a current opening for a Clinical Data Specialist in our Medical Affairs group. This position reports to Senior Director of Clinical Research, and indirectly to the Statistician and Clinical Research Administrator.


The Clinical Data Specialist has the following responsibilities:

  • Participate in the development of data management protocols, case report forms (CRFs) and instructions, queries, procedure/in service manuals, project-specific standard operating procedures, and other data management tools for the assigned clinical trials/registries/research studies.
  • Generate, resolve, and track data queries to assure the integrity of the clinical data with respect to data guidelines and sponsor specifications. Liaise with sites, clinical research staff, and clinical departments, conducting statistical analysis, programming, and data queries, in addition to supporting data transfers and identification and resolution of data management issues.
  • Provide feedback to leadership regarding data collection, entry, and quality control and assurance in the form of standard operating procedures and work instructions for internal use and site use. Detect deficiencies and implement corrective action needed; follow up and completely resolve detected deficiencies.
  • Prepare a variety of narrative and analytical reports and correspondence to Principal and Site investigators and staff; monitor adherence to established timelines and recommend corrective action as necessary.
  • Develop study and educational materials; conduct educational or monitoring visits regarding data management to assure utilization of standardized methodologies for CRF completion; assess adherence to protocols, recommend additional training as needed to sites.
  • Assist in the development of datasets (working to pull in and clean/harmonize data from disparate sources) and prepare data for extraction and analysis. Develop data dictionaries for study team use and for regulatory authorities.
  • Assist in the development and implementation of policies and procedures as needed.

Required Qualifications

  • Organizational, analytical and communications skills
  • Work requires three years of directly related data management experience, preferably in the clinical research field.
