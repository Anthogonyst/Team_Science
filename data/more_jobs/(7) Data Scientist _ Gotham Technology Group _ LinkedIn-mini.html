About the job

Data manipulation and reporting is critical in this role and if you are someone that lives in the data then this role is for you, as it is not a business facing role.


Expectations for your role are:

  • Analyze business initiatives through data
  • Data Manipulation & reporting
  • Investigate and report data inconsistencies and anomalies
  • Perform bulk data management as necessary
  • Build data monitoring dashboards
  • Compare data to innovate new ways of evolving our systems
  • Work closely with Senior Systems Analyst on ad hoc requests
  • Gathering and presenting data to establish the truth about processes and consistency
  • Daily communication with team members to communicate findings
  • Perform queries on data to offer quick insight and help with decision-making
