About the job

In order to capitalize on our success and continued growth plans we are seeking a Cybersecurity Data Scientist in our Information Security group.


As a member of the Information Security team, this role will be taking a lead in using data as the primary vector to identify threats against our business and aid in development of our defense capabilities. The ideal candidate will be knowledgeable in data mining, data science, data analytics, data strategy or data engineering and should be able to design high impacting solutions across the organization. The role will also have an opportunity to work with subject matter experts not only within security, but across infrastructure, network, development, and business teams.


We look to hire people who are comfortable in working with minimal supervision as part of a team that has consistently delivered ground-breaking and innovative solutions in one of the most exciting and fast-moving areas of the of the financial markets. We need people who are able to prioritize and can effectively communicate complex issues to non-technical team members.


Tradeweb Technology jobs are fully remote. The Tradeweb Technology hub is located in our Jersey City office which can be used for team meetings and collaboration efforts. There may be days where travel to the Jersey City office is recommended for organizational off-sites.


Job Responsibilities:

  • Use cybersecurity skill combined with machine learning experience to develop and improve cyber defense capabilities.
  • Utilize Natural Language Processing (NLP) and data from various cyber security tools for anomaly detection.
  • Perform data mining to extract and transform large volumes of data into a format suitable for analysis
  • Build data visualization, metrics and reporting for distribution to senior management and other technical and non-technical teams
  • Create and optimize correlation searches in security information and event management (SIEM) platform
  • Work with security team members to develop behavior-based threat models.


Required Qualifications:

  • 3+ years of experience with data science, data analytics, data strategy or data engineering
  • 3+ years of experience working in an analytical research environment
  • Hands-on programming skills in Python, Java, Powershell or R
  • Demonstrated experience working with SIEM
  • Experience with Splunk development and Splunk Common Information Model (CIM)
  • Strong understanding of Linux and Windows
  • Strong experience in developing SQL statements; knowledge of REGEX
  • Experience in data mining with focus in text mining and pattern discovery.
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
  • Bachelor's degree
  • Certification in data science is a plus
