Job Summary: The role is a biology position in Eternity Bioscience Inc. located in Cranbury, NJ in the biology department.


Essential Job Functions:

  • Perform a variety of in vitro pharmacological (mostly biochemical and cellular) assays to support multiple drug discovery projects, including but not limited to fluorescent, colorimetric or label-free enzymatic and binding kinetics assays, flow cytometry, ELISA, and protein production
  • Deliver high-quality and timely data to project teams
  • Present the work clearly in internal project meetings
  • Be flexible on other lab duties as assigned ad hoc

Job Requirements:

  • BS/MS degree in biological sciences, preferably in cell biology, biochemistry, pharmacology, molecular biology or immunology, with drug discovery research experience desirable
  • Proficiency with data analysis programs, such as Excel and Prism
  • Ability to multitask and adhere to tight timelines
  • Ability to work in a diverse team setting and collaborate effectively on complex projects
  • Technical proficiency, ability to learn new techniques quickly, effective problem solving, and critical and quantitative thinking skills required
  • Excellent communication, interpersonal, and organizational skills
