About the job

Synergy Interactive is partnering with a digital marketing agency to fill a Data Science & Analytics position. This contract position is remote based in NYC and will focus on CRM.


Candidates should be proficient in EComm/CRM KPI frameworks, reporting, insight, and analysis. Day-to-day instruments include SQL queries and Excel Pivot tables. Ability to prepare informative and actionable Powerpoint slides about findings and recommendations is required.


RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Create reporting requirements to drive data capture in the digital CRM experience.
  • Analyze CRM performance data (overall trends, open rates, clickstream, modular analysis, UX) to uncover meaningful insights and actionable recommendations
  • Create and maintain weekly, bi-weekly and monthly reporting.
  • Build ad-hoc analyses as needed to unearth CRM touches, phases or campaign-level learnings
  • Independently prioritize analytical needs of team and business, based on complexity, timing, and visibility
  • Perform basic statistical analysis, such as creating A/B test plans, calculating statistical significance, distributions, etc.
  • Effectively communicate complex ideas to primary client contacts, both verbally and in writing
