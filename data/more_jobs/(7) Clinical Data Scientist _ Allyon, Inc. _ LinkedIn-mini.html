Job Title:



Clinical Data Scientist



Duties & Responsibilities:


=C2=B7 Create input for thought leadership on project methodology with a focus on responsible use of data and data validation.

=C2=B7 Analyze healthcare trends to understand the drivers for our client base in health care outcomes, research, and life sciences domains.

=C2=B7 Will educate to best practices on data driven descriptive and prescriptive analytics, for the purpose of exploring and analyzing the clinical and administrative detail of populations.

=C2=B7 The ideal candidate will have experience working with large data sets, deploying data driven statistical solutions, and has ability to work in an agile environment on new ideas and methods to facilitate customer success and support product design.

=C2=B7 Apply statistical concepts to large data set validations in collaboration with clinical team

=C2=B7 Work closely with customer success teams to understand data and business requirements for healthcare-related use cases

=C2=B7 Select and apply appropriate statistical techniques to analyze clinical and/or other data

=C2=B7 Assist in statistical data interpretation (findings, implications and limitations) and reporting

=C2=B7 Train visualization concepts via ADAMS Platform for analytical visualizations that convert data into insights and actions

=C2=B7 Provide consultation on project design, statistical methodology, statistical analyses and interpretation for customers and partners

=C2=B7 Have strong implementation skills for proposed solutions and work closely with client stakeholders to support training and models deployment

=C2=B7 Support internal and external engagement with both business and technical teams



Minimum Requirements:


=C2=B7 A minimum of 3-5 years research experience in data analysis, statistical and predictive modeling, experimental design, and multivariate analysis

=C2=B7 Prior experience using visualization tools

=C2=B7 Demonstrated proficiency and a minimum of 2+ years experience in python or R programming languages

=C2=B7 Experience working within technical and complex product organizations

=C2=B7 Demonstrated ability to understand healthcare operations, gaps, and opportunities for optimization an advantage

=C2=B7 Familiarity in working with standard healthcare vocabularies, i.e. ICD10, SNOMED, LOINC, etc. an advantage and possess ability to run large scale data validations

=C2=B7 Proven stakeholder management and demonstrated ability to lead interactions with both internal and external clients

=C2=B7 Exceptional ability in interpersonal and communication skills in the ability to clearly describe logic, and implications of a complex statistical models to all types of partners and users

=C2=B7 Ability to mentor other internal and external stakeholders to be able to lead projects from end to end in ADAMS Platform




Other:

=C2=B7 Advanced degree (Phd or Masters) in statistics, biostatistics, mathematics, or data science

=C2=B7 Healthcare biostatistics or clinical or informatics background is an advantage

=C2=B7 Have professional experience conducting research

=C2=B7 Fluency in healthcare data structures, ontologies, research methodology, with great ability to interact with business leaders or customer stakeholders to make recommendations

=C2=B7 Experience in assessing effectiveness and accuracy of data sources

=C2=B7 Ability to travel occasionally as warranted by project work and location; frequency will vary depending on project requirements.
