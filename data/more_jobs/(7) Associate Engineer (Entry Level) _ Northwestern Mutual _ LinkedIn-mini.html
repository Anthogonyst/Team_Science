About the job

At Northwestern Mutual, we are strong, innovative and growing. We invest in our people. We care and make a positive difference.

Associate Engineer (Entry Level)

To support our growing team, we are looking for an Associate Engineer to work with our ICS Reporting & Analytics team. You're the right fit if you love to solve problems and are always striving to learn new things. We're looking for self-motivated and self-driven engineers who are passionate about what they do and love technology. You will be working with our team to build, consolidate, simplify, and support dashboards and reporting tools delivering improved visual and analytical data which drive informed decisions. The team builds collectors and manages data providing visualization and dashboards throughout the company to show progress towards reaching the company's security and DevOps goals. The team is remote with an occasional opportunity to travel to our Milwaukee or New York campuses.

Competencies
  • Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science or equivalent experience
  • Familiarity with Agile methodologies/DevOps, continuous integration, continuous deployment (CICD) beneficial
  • Fast learner and self-starter. The people you will work with are willing to share knowledge and lend a helping hand, but you must be willing to take initiative, work with minimal supervision, and actively interact with other team members
  • Bring new ideas and new ways of solving problems to the team
  • Ability to research, perform quantitative analysis
  • Building and maintaining dashboards and reports to quickly visualize actionable insights and effective decisions
  • Continuously improve dashboards and data by applying standards and responding to feedback
  • Proactively learn emerging platforms and related technology
  • Use of existing procedures to solve standard problems; analyze information and standard practices to make judgements
  • Work within standardized procedures & practices to achieve objectives and meet deadlines
Experience
  • Bachelor's Degree or equivalent experience
  • Proficient in at least one programming language (Python, Go, Rust, Typescript, JavaScript, Java)
  • API development and web app development a plus
  • Database and SQL experience a plus
  • Source Control Management (Git), Public Cloud experience (AWS), containerization (Docker) experience all a plus
  • Dashboard / Reporting Tools (Grafana, PowerBI) a plus
  • Ability to articulate opinions to team members and share ideas, good verbal/written communications
  • Self-learner: willingly immerse yourself in technology to obtain new skills, insights and certification
  • Certification in Cloud Platform (AWS) or DevOps technology advantageous
Benefits
  • Tuition reimbursement, commuter plans, and paid time off
  • Highly competitive compensation that include base salary plus bonus
  • Medical/Dental/Vision plans, 401(k), pension program, and more!
