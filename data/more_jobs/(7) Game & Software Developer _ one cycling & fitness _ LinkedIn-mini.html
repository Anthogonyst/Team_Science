About the job

one cycling & fitness is gamifying wearable biometric data for team-based interval workouts. Using heart-rate technology as the metrics for the workout, our app provides users an immersive game experience.


The COVID-19 pandemic changed the face of the fitness industry. A reliance on technology became the norm. As gyms and studios return to brick-and-mortar operation, a hybrid style of fitness will replace what was once a divided marketplace. Gamification of fitness has been steadily increasing in popularity and will now take a more prominent role in the industry's future. Collectively, the fitness and video game industries command a $130 billion market capitalization.


one cycling & fitness aims to be the spearhead of gamified hybrid fitness, delivering a virtual model that can also be enjoyed in-studio. one cycling & fitness is based in Houston with plans to franchise the studio concept nationwide.


To this end, we are seeking a GAME DESIGNER / SOFTWARE DEVELOPER. Within parameters provided by the CEO and under the supervision of the Team Lead, the successful candidate will have creative flexibility in the design and implementation of the interface, base engine, animation and graphics.


Skills required include:

  • Unity 4 or 5 Engine
  • C# coding
  • Multi-networking using C# and Unity
  • Writing and testing C# code in Unity
  • Source code control management platforms (Github/Sourcetree)
  • iOS, macOS, Android mobile app development
  • UI/UX design, implementation, integration


Knowledge or experience (personal or professional) with the fitness industry is a strong plus.


Attributes sought:

  • Good communication skills
  • Team player
  • Proactive self-starter
  • Analytical/problem-solving
  • Attention-to-detail
  • Organized thought and workflow
