About the job

The position requires the ability to support multiple projects in all development roles, mentor more junior staff, and apply technology expertise. Software Engineers are able to manage an entire project in their respective area and they possess a technical competency within their area (i.e. applications development, Network/Systems Administration, Business/Financial/Clinical Systems). Additionally, Software Engineers must have the ability to negotiate the products and deliverables of a project. They are also able to prioritize the tasks of a projects time-line to achieve the desired departmental and end-user requirements. They share their expertise for the good of the organization by mentoring, coaching and giving general direction to other teammates. They generally hold a Bachelors Degree in computer science or other related field and commonly have 5+ years experience.


"must-haves" - .NET, C#


1. Bachelors degree required, MS preferred or PhD in bioinformatics, computer science and/or the life sciences and 0-2 years relevant experience.

2. Back end and API development using Python, NodeJS, C#, Java, R, Perl.

3. SQL proficiency using MySQL and/or SqlServer

4. Web development using Angular 6+, Python Flask (ASP.NET would be a plus).

5. Familiarity SDLC principles.

6. Strong interpersonal skills and ability to effectively interact with all levels of staff and external contacts.

7. Excellent analytical, organizational and time management skills.
