About the job

How would you like to become a Machine Learning Engineer at one of the leading reinsurance companies headquartered in NYC. This is an excellent opportunity to have a tremendous amount of autonomy and ability to effect real change at the company.


What's The Job?

As our Machine Learning Engineer, you'll work closely with two (2) other ML Engineers on the team. You will be building ML Models from end to end and pushing into production. Build the model, back test the model, train the model, optimize the model. You will be working on projects that effect real change at the company, your models will help define the future strategy of the company. This is a highly visible role, part of a highly visible team. You will be reporting directly to the Head of Data.


This role sits in our amazing NYC office and will operate on a hybrid model of 2-3 days in the office and remote.


Who Are We?

We are a leading reinsurance organization headquartered in NYC. We are approximately 650 teammates strong, and have offices all over the world. We value Data and Data Science implementation, and know the importance it plays in our future success.


What Skills Do You Need?

You have built from end to end, and pushed into production, ML Models previously.

Python is your #1 go-to.

You have hands on experience with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, Azure)

Experience with Big Data, Spark / Databricks is a nice plus.

We find candidates with a Quant / Statistician background do well here.

We like candidates how have activate GitHub accounts and/or are Kaggle competitors.
