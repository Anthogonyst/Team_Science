About the job

We are a multi- billion dollar alternative investment firm in midtown Manhattan that is focused on managing assets for our global clients. We are comprised of some of the most sophisticated and experienced investment individuals on the buy-side. As a fund to fund hedge fund we currently have 20 - 30 different hedge funds in our portfolio ranging from long/short term funds to crypto. We have been around for nearly 20 years and with close to 5.5 billion under management.


What Is The Job?

As an Investment Associate you will work closely with our investment team to support manager and strategy research, investment due diligence, risk management, and portfolio management. You will create analytical reports for the investment team and other internal stakeholders, organize investment data and oversee investment data entry and processing.

You will also work closely with the software development team to create and automate tech tools for analysis that are used to do a first pass on their investments (evaluate risk, potential numbers etc...) You will maintain help to maintain these tools and suggest and help build new tools that you deem necessary moving forward. This is paramount as you will be the primary end user of these tools hence the close partnership!
