About the job

**Candidates will either need to possess a Public Trust clearance or begin the application process (~2 months) in order to obtain both the aforementioned clearance and the position available.**


**Candidates must be fully vaccinated.**


Job Description:

We are seeking a Software Engineer to work on the team that provides integrated water prediction, forecasting the movement and presence of water throughout the nation in terms of quantity and availability, from summit to sea, treetop to bedrock, with the appropriate timeliness, resolution, reliability, accuracy, and quantified uncertainty required to inform risk-based water resource management decisions.


Responsibilities:

  • Design and code software components, units, and modules that meet product specifications and development schedules.
  • Tests and debugs assigned components and units.
  • Participates in large system and subsystem planning.
  • Assist in "Research to Operations" efforts, deploying the latest science in water prediction into a reliable operational environment.
  • Assist computational scientists in bridging the gap between applied research and software engineering; supporting mathematical modeling, numerical analysis, algorithm development, analysis, validation, and visualization of results.


Qualifications:

  • 2+ years of experience as a software engineer (utilizing: Python, R, or C#)
  • Experience with Docker
  • Experience with GitHub
  • Bachelor's degree in engineering, computer science, or related field
