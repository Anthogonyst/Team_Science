About the job

Responsibilities

  • Provide coding support (primarily in R) to Pharmacokineticists conducting PK, PD, Toxicokinetic, Population PK analyses, etc. related to drug development
  • Support clinical pharmacology experts in the design and interpretation of Phase 1 clinical studies in healthy volunteers or special populations (e.g., hepatic and renal impaired, etc.)
  • Assist with designing programming code to generate summary PK and PD tables, listings, and graphs from clinical and nonclinical data
  • Assist with the development of code to clean and summarize data from nonclinical and clinical studies, including implementation of CDISC (SDTM, ADaM, and SEND) standards
  • Develop well commented template code that can be reused across multiple studies and scenarios by multiple programmers
  • Work independently to provide complex programming support across the company and for Clients
  • Participate in the development of and ensure compliance with standard operating procedures and guidelines
  • Manage and lead projects with minimal guidance
