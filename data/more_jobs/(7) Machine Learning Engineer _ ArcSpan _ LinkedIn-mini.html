We are seeking a collaborative, innovative Machine Learning Engineer to join our team!


The Machine Learning (ML) Engineer will develop a suite of machine learning and deep learning solutions for the ArcSpan Audience Engine product platform. The position involves working with the product team to build publisher look-alike models, bid floor pricing models and optimization algorithms that will in turn enable ArcSpan customers to better optimize their revenue. The ML Engineer will report to the SVP of Engineering.


The successful candidate will have previously worked as a data scientist/ML Engineer driving product solutions within a complex, fast-paced environment and preferably at a tech-focused startup.
