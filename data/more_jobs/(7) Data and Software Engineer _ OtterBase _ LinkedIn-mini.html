About the job

Help advance the analytics capabilities of our organization thru engineering the data lake, data warehouse, and data marts and integrating that data into dashboard products


You will collaborate with peers on multiple analytics projects and help use data to drive operational efficiency, identify unique insights, and help answer critical questions


As a self-motivated team member, you will work with the data team and leaders to understand and develop analytical approaches to support our mission. Activities include:

  • collection
  • analysis and visualization of data
  • integration of data into pipelines and ETL processes
  • preparation of actionable reports
  • troubleshooting data integrity and analysis issues


The Data Engineer and Software Developer will continually search for opportunities to streamline processes and understand the needs of stakeholders, both internal and external
