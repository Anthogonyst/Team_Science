About the job

We believe communication belongs to everyone. We exist to democratize phone service. TextNow is evolving the way the world connects and that's because we're made up of people with curious minds who bring an optimistic, yet critical lens into the work we do. We're the largest provider of free phone service in the nation. And we're just getting started.

Join us in our mission to break down barriers to communication and free the flow of conversation for people everywhere.

TextNow's backend Trust and Safety team uses cutting edge technologies in a hybrid micro-services environment. Some additional prominent technologies include Kubernetes, gRPC, high throughput databases, streaming and queueing. We manage hundreds of millions of user interactions and terabytes of raw data in real-time. We are looking for software developers to expand our capabilities in harnessing this data to protect our customers. As a Senior Backend Developer, you will mentor Backend Developers, gain real world experience in building scalable, high performing systems that integrate with both heuristic and machine learning models creating an industry leading solution for affordable telecommunications.

What You'll Do
  • Work as a member of the team that owns the architectural strategy for our platform, as we build a modern, reliable, scalable and safe site
  • Architectural design and supporting code rewrites related to the messaging platform, distributed job system, caches, and data platforms
  • Build and own the microservices on the Trust and Safety team as well as cross functional concerns of these services
  • Maintain and refactor services such as our internal fraud management service and/or vendor integrations
  • Proactively improve services per defined industry best practices e.g. refactoring, testing and documentation best practices
  • Production problem investigation and resolution
  • Performance diagnostics and tuning
  • Own RCA and incident mitigations and standards
  • Provide technical leadership to feature teams with respect to security best practices
  • Partner with Data Science, Data Engineering, Site Reliability Engineering, and Support
  • Participate as lead in an on-call rotation. Each engineer does 1 week every 2 months, paired with a second backend engineer
