About the job

About the Role

The software you create will be used to manage our cloud service and supportive tooling. While working in a fast-paced environment with smart team members, you'll be responsible for understanding business requirements and constraints, converting requirements into viable software, and exposing APIs for application developers.

Responsibilities


  • Convert business requirements and constraints into technically viable software.
  • Develop new libraries, tools, applications, and process for rapid development of features across a suite of integrated applications.
  • Diagram and document the software architecture.
  • Work within a team of other engineers, business analysts, and researchers.
  • Monitor and respond to system events to support customer success.
  • Candidate must be able to work independently and bring tasks to completion


Requirements

A successful candidate will have the follow qualities:

  • Experience writing production applications in Go, Python, or C#
  • Experience in SQL database concepts, designs, and optimizations
  • Experience building HTTP APIs using REST, gRPC, or GraphQL


Preferred Experience

  • Knowledge of cloud computing environments (Azure, AWS)
  • Experience handling imagery and large data stores
  • Knowledge of data warehousing concepts and methodologies
  • Experience with robotics or machine learning
  • Experience with tools (git, static analyzers, linters) to manage code and improve code quality
  • Exposure to a breadth of software architectures and deployments (cloud, on-premise, mobile, desktop, edge)
  • BS, MS, or PhD in Computer Science, Software Engineering, Data Science, Data Engineering, or closely related computing field


Next steps

Work Hours and Benefits

As part of the Airtonomy team, your time will be spent working both independently and as a group to complete critical projects and develop solutions to advance the goals of the company and its customers. Work hours are flexible and regulated by the projects and work product required by your team and team supervisor. Benefits include unlimited leave per the terms outlined in our policy (sick and vacation) in addition to health, dental, and vision insurance.
