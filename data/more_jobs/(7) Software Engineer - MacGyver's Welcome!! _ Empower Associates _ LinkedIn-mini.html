We are looking to add new Software Engineers to help in supporting an external software platform that helps run some of the largest financial companies in North America. This role is fully remote, has a flexible schedule, and gives the opportunity to work around an extremely talented engineering team.


This role will see you supporting a big data/database product that puts unlimited power in the hands of its users. Anyone in the organization can control billions of rows of data with ease. It is an incredibly powerful Java application that is capable of running in almost any environment you throw it's way (Linux, Windows, MacOS, On-Prem/Cloud, Containers, Etc..) It's users can interact with the product using whichever language they prefer (Groovy, Python, Java, C++, R, C#, and SQL), and it is capable of making companies millions of dollars when it is implemented correctly.


This is where you come in! The role will be customer-facing, and guarantees to never pigeonhole you into a single type of work. It will see you spending your time managing a variety of projects that will include; building features, fixing bugs, customizing reports/dashboards, performance tuning the application, and interacting with external client teams.


The ideal candidate will have some, or all, of the following qualifications:

  • Deep curiosity and interest in learning new things.
  • Self-driven, self-organized, proactive, highly motivated, personable, and intelligent.
  • Deep interest in complex software systems.
  • Obsession with producing quality results.
  • Exceptional troubleshooting and problem-solving skills.
  • Superb debugging skills.
  • Programming experience in multiple languages (e.g., Java, C++, C#, Python).
  • Excellent math skills.
  • Understanding of algorithmic complexity.
  • Experience with statistical software (e.g., R, Matlab, Mathematica, Python, etc.)
  • Experience with a Command Line Interface.
  • Some experience with database software.
  • Basic understanding of threaded and distributed computing.
  • Strong analytical and organizational skills.
  • Detail Oriented.
